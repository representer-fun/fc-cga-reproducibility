# CIKM Mechanism Analysis

- integration: closest held-out support coverage is Random feasible; hardest generated examples are Typology projected.
- layering: closest held-out support coverage is Plausible typology; hardest generated examples are Typology projected.

Interpretation: a CIKM story is stronger when projected/typology-aware variants are both ledger-valid and closer to held-out positives than generic feasible samples, while avoiding the largest profile drift.
